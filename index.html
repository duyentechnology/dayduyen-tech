Create QR Codes That Preserve What Matters

More than just a link. Duyên QR codes connect physical moments to lasting digital memories. Perfect for businesses who want to add meaning to their products, and individuals who want to preserve the stories behind life's special moments.

Who Uses DUYÊN?

Ways businesses and individuals preserve what matters

🌹

Florists & Gift Shops

Include a QR code on gift cards. Recipients scan to watch a video of you arranging the bouquet, read the sender's message, and get care instructions.

"When the flowers fade, the memory doesn't."

👕

E-commerce & Fashion

Add QR codes to product tags. Share your sustainability story, fabric origins, care guides, and repair services—meeting California SB 707 requirements.

"Turn disposable fashion into cherished items with stories."

☕

Restaurants & Cafes

Table QR codes that customers can save. They preserve the memory of a special date night, anniversary dinner, or coffee shop where they wrote their novel.

"Places become part of your story."

✂️

Service Providers

Hair stylists, nail artists, photographers—give clients a QR card with before/after photos, care tips, and your contact info for their next appointment.

"Stay connected beyond the appointment."

🎨

Makers & Artisans

Include QR codes with handmade items. Share your process, materials, story, and care instructions. Turn your craft into a cherished heirloom.

"Your story makes your work priceless."

💝

Individuals & Families

Create memory cards for special gifts. Add photos, voice messages, and stories that the recipient can keep forever—long after the physical gift is gone.

"Gifts become memories, memories become tapestries."

How DUYÊN Works

From creation to lasting memory in four simple steps

1

Generate Your QR Code

Create a unique QR code in seconds. Customize with your colors, add your business name, and choose from Avery label templates for easy printing.

2

Attach to Your Product or Card

Print on stickers, cards, tags, or packaging. Each QR code is unique and links to a beautiful landing page on duyen.io.

3

Add Your Content

Upload photos, videos, messages, care instructions, or contact information. Create the experience you want recipients to discover.

4

Recipient Scans & Saves

When someone scans your QR code, they see your beautiful story. They can save it to their personal Duyên archive—a private collection they can search and cherish forever.

About Duyên

Duyên was born in the quiet moments between destinations. While driving for Lyft during the pandemic, my passengers carried flowers, gifts, wrapped boxes — proof that someone, somewhere, was thinking of someone else. Bouquets to anniversaries. Care packages to new parents. A single rose to a doorstep in Oakland.

Every ride, I watched love show up. And every ride, I watched it disappear. The flowers wilted. The cards got lost. The love was real — why does the evidence have to be temporary?

In Vietnamese, duyên is the invisible thread that explains why certain people, places, and things enter your life and stay. Not quite fate, not quite chance. The reason your grandmother's ring feels like more than gold. Why a stranger's kindness, years later, still sits warm in your chest.

That is why Duyên exists. We give meaningful connections a place to live.

Scan a card tucked into a bouquet and you don't just see who sent it — you hear their voice, read their message, save the moment. Scan a tag on a handmade jacket and you see where it was made, who made it, how to repair it. Add your own photos, your own memories. When it wears out, you don't throw it away — the pattern lives, the story continues.

Every moment you save becomes another thread. We're building it all for your life tapestry — woven from the people, places, and things that made you who you are.

Not everything is chance. Some things are meant to be woven in.
Duyên is where they stay.

— Trinh Dao
Founder, Duyên
